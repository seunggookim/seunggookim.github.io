## Behavioral characterization
Indifferent pitch height confusion from non-AP musicians

No difference in frequency discrimination from non-AP musicians.

*Thesis*: The absolute recognition of pitch chroma is a categorization based on an internal template of pitch chroma. 

## Microstructure characterization
Higher intracortical myelination in the anterior part of the superior temporal plane (planum polare).

## Resting-state functional connectivity characterization
Also higher RSFC from the highly myelinated area to the bilateral STS. Need more samples.
