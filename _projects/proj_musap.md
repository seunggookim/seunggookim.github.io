## 1. Behavioral characterization
While it has been characterized by "the accurate recogniztion of pitch chroma without an external reference", there seem to be multiple correlating behavioral characteristics, which would sharpen the definition of absolute pitch.

<h5>1.1. Indifferent pitch height confusion from non-AP musician</h5>
The degrees of octave confusion was irrelevant to the accuity of chroma recognition.

<h5>1.2. No difference in frequency discrimination from non-AP musicians</h5>

<h5>1.3. The absolute recognition of pitch chroma seems a categorization based on an internal template of pitch chroma</h5>
