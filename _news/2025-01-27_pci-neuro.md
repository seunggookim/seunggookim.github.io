As of 26 Janurary 2025, I have joined the Peer Communitiy In Neuroscience [(PCI-neuro)](https://neuro.peercommunityin.org/) as a Recommender, which functions similarly to a handling editor in conventional journals.
The "Peer Community In" [(PCI)](https://peercommunityin.org) is a non-for-profit and non-commerical organization that publishes peer reviews in open access at no cost.
Once a preprint is recommended by a Recommender based on the reviews, authors can choose send it to PCI-friendly journals for an expedited processing.
It would attract more authors with more journals that do not require additional reviews with a recommendation.
Let's see how this radical movement (by scientists, for scientitsts!🧪) unfolds.
The Max Planck Society is one of the supporting societies.
